### Wachtenlab

**Wachtenlab** is the GitHub organization of the **Prof. Dr. Dagmar Wachten Lab** at the *Institute of Innate Immunity, University of Bonn*.

This space is dedicated to **maintaining and sharing analysis code** that supports reproducible, quantitative life science research.

## Scientific background

The Wachtenlab studies **cellular communication and signaling**, with a particular emphasis on:

- Primary cilia and ciliary signaling  
- Immune and non-immune cell interactions  
- Optogenetics and biosensor development  
- Quantitative imaging and biophysical approaches  

The code shared here supports these research areas but may also be useful beyond them.
